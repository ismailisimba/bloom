Psychology

We aim to explain how thoughts, emotions, and behaviours change and effect our development across three major dimensions: physical, cognitive and psychosocial development.

Our specialists include a wide array of Psychologists from various fields including Development, Clinical, Counselling, and Psychiatry. We tend to clients of all ages and cater to various psychological related cases.

Psychological services we provide include Assessments (screening for psychological disorders, psychometric tests, personality, neurocognitive and intelligence testing), Counselling, Psychotherapy and Workshops.

Services We Provide:

Counselling: Fosters clients through milestones, and transition stages in their lives. It entails helping individuals and/or families find a balance in their lives, teaching them resources to deal with various stressors in life, establishing goals and motivating them to reflect and recognize their resources.
