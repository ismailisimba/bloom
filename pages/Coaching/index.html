Coaching

We support our clients in unlocking their potential and maximize their opportunities by exploring their personal identity and teaching them how to use it as a tool of actions for their progress. We offer Life coaching (psychology based) and Career/ Personal development coaching.

Life Coaching: This is an individual-based service that is specific and goal-oriented. Unlike therapy, we do not ‘diagnose’ and ‘treat’ our clients. Life coaching aims to support smooth life transitions by teaching clients how to identify, address, and overcome life stressors.

Career Coaching: This is an individual- or group-based service to help clients define, redefine, and achieve professional goals and/or other work-related plans. It is rooted in motivation for professional growth and development.

Our approach is not to resolve things for you but to equip you with the necessary tools to develop skills so that you can resolve them independently.
